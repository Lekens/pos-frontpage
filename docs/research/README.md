## Target Audience

### Primary — Store Owners & Managers (Decision Makers)

| Segment | Description | Pain Points |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| **Supermarkets** | 50–500 SKUs, 2–10 cashiers | Manual stock tracking, no insights, paper receipts |
| **Mini-marts / Provision stores** | 100–1000 SKUs, 1–3 cashiers | Lost inventory, no reports, cash reconciliation errors |
| **Pharmacies** | Expiry tracking critical | Manual expiry monitoring, wrong stock counts |
| **Electronics/Hardware stores** | High-value items | Theft risk, no audit trail |
| **Fashion/Clothing boutiques** | Seasonal inventory | No variant tracking, manual calculations |

### Secondary — Cashiers (End Users)
- Need speed and simplicity
- Often not tech-savvy
- Must use the system daily with minimal training

### Geography
- Primary: Nigeria (Tier 1 — Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt; Tier 2 — Kano, Ibadan, Kaduna)
- Secondary: Ghana, Kenya, South Africa (future expansion)

## Pricing Strategy

### Single Plan: ₦49,999 / month

**What's included:**
- Unlimited cashiers
- Unlimited products and categories
- All 4 payment methods (Cash, Bank Transfer, POS/Card, Credit)
- Web + Desktop app (Windows/macOS)
- Mobile cashier app (iOS/Android)
- 24/7 access to sales reports and inventory
- WhatsApp expiry alerts (admin phone)
- Loyalty programme for customers
- Transaction charge rules
- Stock adjustment + purchase invoice management
- Up to 3 store locations (additional ₦10,000/month per extra store)
- Email support

**Why ₦49,999:**
- Cheaper than one cashier's monthly salary
- Less than one major stock loss event
- Pays for itself if it prevents 2 theft incidents or stockout errors per month
