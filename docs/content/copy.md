## FAQ

```
Q1: Do I need special hardware?
A1: No special hardware required. POS-Choice works on any computer, tablet,
    or smartphone. For thermal printing, any USB 58mm or 80mm ESC/POS printer
    works (available from ₦8,000 in Nigerian markets).

Q2: What happens when internet goes down?
A2: The desktop app has offline mode — transactions are saved locally and
    synced when internet returns. The web app requires an internet connection.
    The mobile app can hold transactions offline.

Q3: Can I use it on my phone?
A3: Yes! The POS-Choice mobile app (iOS & Android) lets cashiers make full
    sales from a smartphone — including barcode scanning with the camera.
    Perfect for stores without a dedicated computer.

Q4: How many cashiers can I have?
A4: Unlimited. Each cashier gets their own PIN code and you can see their
    individual performance on the admin dashboard — transactions, revenue,
    discounts given, and more.

Q5: What payment methods are supported?
A5: Cash, bank transfer (customer pays via mobile banking), POS/card terminal,
    store credit (for trusted customers), and split payments across multiple
    methods. Transaction fees are configurable (e.g. add ₦50 for transfers above ₦10k).

Q6: How do I import my existing products?
A6: Upload an Excel file (.xlsx) with your product list. The system
    automatically imports names, prices, barcodes, and categories.
    No technical knowledge needed.

Q7: Is my data safe?
A7: Yes. All data is stored on encrypted servers with daily backups.
    Your data is never shared with third parties. You can export all
    your data at any time. See our Privacy Policy.

Q8: What if I have multiple branches?
A8: Up to 3 stores are included in the standard plan. Additional stores
    are ₦10,000/month each. Each branch has its own stock, cashiers,
    and reports — but you can view everything from a single admin dashboard.

Q9: How does the WhatsApp notification work?
A9: You set your WhatsApp number in the settings. When a product is within
    30 days of expiry (configurable) and still has stock, the system
    sends you a WhatsApp message automatically — no apps to install.

Q10: Can I cancel anytime?
A10: Yes. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel any month
     and your subscription ends at the next billing date. Your data is
     kept for 30 days after cancellation.
```
