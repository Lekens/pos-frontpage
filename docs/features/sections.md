## Section 10 — FAQ

1. **Do I need special hardware?** — No special hardware required. Works on any computer, tablet, or smartphone. For thermal printing, any USB 58mm or 80mm thermal printer works (starting from ₦8,000).

2. **What happens when internet goes down?** — The desktop app has offline mode. Transactions are saved locally and synced when internet returns. The web app requires internet.

3. **Can I use it on my phone?** — Yes! The mobile cashier app (iOS & Android) lets you make full sales from your smartphone. Perfect for stores without a dedicated computer.

4. **How many cashiers can I have?** — Unlimited. Each cashier gets their own PIN code. You can view their individual performance on the admin dashboard.

5. **What payment methods does it support?** — Cash, bank transfer (customer pays via mobile banking), POS/card terminal, and store credit for trusted customers. Split payments are also supported.

6. **How do I import my existing products?** — Upload an Excel file (.xlsx) with your product list. The system imports names, prices, barcodes, and categories automatically.

7. **Is my data safe?** — Yes. Data is stored on encrypted servers with daily backups. Soft-delete protects against accidental deletion. You can export all your data at any time.

8. **What if I have multiple branches?** — Up to 3 stores on the standard plan. Additional stores are ₦10,000/month each. Each branch has its own stock, cashiers, and reports.

9. **How does the WhatsApp notification work?** — You set your WhatsApp number in the settings. When a product is within 30 days of expiry (and still in stock), the system sends you a WhatsApp message automatically.

10. **Can I cancel anytime?** — Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel any month and your subscription ends at the next billing date.
